About us

MHP Thermal Engineers is a well known name in the thermal industry. MTE is engaged in turnkey Projects for Fabrication & Erection of Boiler House Accessories to industrial houses and also high value domestic installations, needy customers who need all services under one roof not only within the country only but internationally too.

Field of activities

  • Fabrication of Chimney, Tanks, Ducting, Bag Filter, Wet Scrubber, Expansion Tanks, APH and Cyclomax etc.
  • Shifting and Erection of Heavy Equipment such as Boilers, Chimney and Bag Filter etc.
  • Repair of APH, Cyclomax, Boiler Tubes etc.
  • Material Handling system i.e. Belt Conveyer, Coal Crusher, Coal handling equipment, Bucket Elevator etc.
  • All type of pipe line such as IBR/NIBR and SS etc.
  • All Type of Bag Filter/ Dust Collectors, Cyclomax.
