Thermic fluid heater

A Thermic fluid heater is industrial heating equipment, used where only heat transfers are desired instead of pressure. In this equipment, a thermic fluid is circulated in the entire system for heat transfers to the desired processes. MHP Thermal Engineers has installed hundreds of thermic heating system equipment for various clients in India and have made them operational after the completion of all legal paper work.MHP Thermal offers services in installation and assembly of thermal oil/thermic fluid heaters, fired by a wide range of fuels. Our engineering team has expertise in both solid fuel firing as well as oil/gas firing versions so that you get the advantage of all services under one roof.

  • Installation and Assembly: the entire assembly of a thermic fluid heater after manufacturing contains a heat transfer fluid, a heating element – an electric element, and one of the boundary walls of this fluid is also a heat exchange surface with the heat consumer. The whole system should be considered as a heat exchange system without an intermediate system or circuit, and in which there does not strictly exist any carrier fluid that performs only energy “transfer” functions, but a fluid as a contact medium and which, therefore, shares more similarities, especially regarding difficulties and disadvantages, with direct heating.
  • Maintenance: Thermic Fluid systems are extremely expensive and valuable assets for industry, its maintenance is critical to the effectiveness and safety of Thermic Fluid System. Routine monitoring of thermic fluid condition is a critical part of ongoing Thermic Fluid System maintenance as its degradation is a complicated chemical process, laboratory techniques can provide rapid insights into the status of a Thermic Fluid and Thermic Fluid system. Foreign contamination like moisture, environmental and system build contaminants are the major cause of degradation, which act to accelerate the ageing of a Thermic Fluid. Getting the maintenance done of the Thermic Fluid Heater is highly necessary for any plant, so that the plant efficiency and production do not affect and this is a responsibility of its operator to monitor it timely.
